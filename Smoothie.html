Tropical Fruit Smoothie

A tropical smoothie blend of ripe mango, strawberries, papaya, orange juice, and ice!

Ingredients

  • 1 mango, peeled and seeded
  • 1 papaya, peeled and seeded
  • ½ cup fresh strawberries
  • ⅓ cup orange juice
  • 5 cubes ice

    Steps

  1. Combine mango, papaya, strawberries, orange juice, and ice cubes in a blender; blend until smooth.
